SUNDAY SCHOOL


Sunday School is available for all ages, every Sunday morning from 9:30 to 10:20.

Children study in English in 4 age appropriate classes.
Preschool: 0-4 years
Lower Elementary: 5-6 years
Middle Elementary: 7-9 years
Upper Elementary: 10-11 years

Youth study in English in 3 classes.
Middle School: 12-14 years
High School: 15-16 years
College: 17+ years

Adults study in Vietnamese in 2 classes.
Men
Women

English Speaking Adults are welcome to join our English College class.

New believer classes are also offered in Vietnamese & English.
