CHILDREN'S MINISTRY


Children's Ministry is a priority for our church. In addition to Sunday School we offer the following programs.

Children's Church Service

Birth to 11 years.

Our children join in singing with the adults & youth in our main worship service.
After singing, the children are dismissed to an age-appropriate service.

Friday Night Classes

During our Friday night Bible Study, we offer two classe for children to study the Bible on their own level.

Annual VBS

Every Summer we host a 3-day Vacation Bible School for the children, using exciting themes that allow the children to have fun and learn important Bible truths at the same time.

Playground

Every Sunday our children enjoy playing in the afternoon while the parents fellowship together.

We also have several annual events for children such as a Tet (Asian New Year) Carnival & a Moon Festival.

YOUTH & YOUNG ADULTS MINISTRY


Youth & Young Adults & are the next generation of the church. Our youth & young adults ministires are designed to empower our young people to know & serve God with all of their hearts. In addition to Sunday School we offer:

Youth Worship Service

Friday Nights @ 7:30 PM

Middle School, High School, & College
We worship in English with a contemporary style.

A youth appropriate message from God's Word with practical application is delivered each week.

Annual Youth Convention & Summer Camp

Twice a year our youth go away for a weekend dedicated to fun, fellowship, and growing in their Christian walk. This is a highlight of the year for many of our youth.

Ministry Opportunities

Our worship team & band on both Friday nights & Sunday mornings include many of our youth.

We participate in the NC District Fine Arts Festival each year where our youth can develop many skills & compete with other youth from around the state.

Our youth choir & drama team prepare songs & skits for special occasions at the church.

Many of our youth also participate in children's ministry helping with our Summer VBS & throughout the year.

We have even brought our youth to serve another church in presenting a Summer Sports Camp for urban children.

Friendship

Every Sunday afternoon our youth take advantage of our facilities to play together soccer, football, volleyball, basketball, ping pong, and more.
